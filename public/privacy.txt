CHAT FOR FUN - PRIVACY POLICY
Last Updated: January 2026

1. DATA COLLECTION: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. 
2. EPHEMERAL CHATS: All messages, images, and voice notes are processed in real-time memory and are never saved to a database. 
3. SESSION DATA: Your temporary profile (Alias, Age, Gender, Bio) exists only for the duration of your active session and is wiped instantly upon disconnection.
4. COOKIES: We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.
